> All challenges from @Hack 2026.

## About @Hack 2026 🛸

@Hack 2026 is the 3rd edition of @Hack - a Canadian Capture The Flag (CTF) competition, hosted
by [HEXPLOIT ALLIANCE](https://hexploit-alliance.com/) and [TECHNATION](https://technationcanada.ca/) at Concordia
University in Montreal.

- **Location**: Concordia University, Montreal
- **Dates**: March 7th-8th, 2026
- **Prizes**: $16K in cash (💰) + nearly $6K in total sponsor prizes (🎁)
- **Results**: [Check the full scoreboard on CTFTime](https://ctftime.org/event/3027)
